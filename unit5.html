🔍 Discover the Rule
❓ لاحظ الفرق بين الجمل دي:
🔹 There is — لما أتكلم عن حاجة واحدة (مفرد)
✅ "There is a kitchen" = فيه مطبخ.
🔹 There are — لما أتكلم عن أكتر من حاجة (جمع)
✅ "There are two bedrooms" = فيه غرفتي نوم.
🔹 Is there? — لما أسأل عن وجود حاجة
✅ "Is there a garden?" = في حديقة؟
🧠 Grammar Lab – There is / There are
➕ Singular
There is a bed.
There's a couch.
➕ Plural
There are two chairs.
There are some books.
➖ Negative
There isn't a TV.
There aren't any plants.
❓ Question
Is there a bathroom?
Are there any windows?
Yes, there is. / No, there aren't.
