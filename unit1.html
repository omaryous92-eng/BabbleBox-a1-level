🧠 Grammar Lab

قاعدة فعل الكينونة (Verb to be):

➕ الإثبات (Positive)
  • I am ...
  • He / She / It is ...
  • You / We / They are ...
➖ النفي (Negative)
  • I'm not ...
  • He / She / It isn't ...
  • You / We / They aren't ...
❓ السؤال (Question)
  • Am I ...?
  • Is he / she / it ...?
  • Are you / we / they ...?
✅ الإجابة القصيرة (Short Answer)
  • Yes, I am. / No, I'm not.
